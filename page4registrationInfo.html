Tipu - Landscape & Gardening Expo 2020 emphasizes on the importance of plants. Bringing together various elements that help one create flourishing gardens.

Your ticket includes visiting all the stalls at the Event. For all the visitors free parking is available at the venue. A printed ticket or showing the barcode on your smartphone to the entry staff is required. If you can’t attend for whatever reason, the ticket is non-refundable, but it is transferrable so you can give the ticket to a friend. There is a student discount available for the Learning sessions conducted at the Event.
