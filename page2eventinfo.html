About the event

This Event is perfect for who are planning renovation projects
and changes to their garden.

BOOK NOW

Date and Time:

2nd - 3rd May 2020, 09:00am to 07:00pm

Entry Ticket Price:

15$ for Adults
5$ for Children & Senior Citizens

Venue:

Orakei Bay, 231 Orakei Rd, Remuera, Auckland

Organizer:

Tipu Pvt. Ltd. (186A Henderson Valley Road, Henderson, Auckland 0612)

The participants include leading nurseries, gardening & landscape societies and suppliers of the gardening equipment, seeds, fertilizers etc from different cities including Wellington, Christchurch, Hamilton, Dunedin and Tauranga, apart from nurseries from the Auckland. Special themed gardens are developed by various gardening & landscaping service providers. A large variety of seasonal and exclusive flowers will be displayed. This event is ideal to meet manufacturers, traders and service providers. For all the professionals in this sector, it is very useful, amazing and a productive experience. There are over 100 stalls, which offers wide variety of options to choose for garden enthusiasts. Apart from this there will be a variety of food stalls for visitors.

There will be separate sections to sell the plants, gardening equipment, pesticides, fertilizers and other general items etc. This will provide an added advantage for the visitors to buy gardening equipment and saplings under one roof.

This is one of the significant events on renovators, developers, contractors, dealers, eco-friendly product, agencies gardening, home, decoration, lifestyle, garden design and house decoration areas.

The Highlights of this Expo:

  • Display of Terrariums, Ornamental Plants, Potted Plants, Foliage, Medicinal & Herbal Plants, Indoor Plants, Hanging Baskets, Dahlia, Roses, Cacti, Bonsai, Bougainvillea, Various Bulbs, Seeds, Cut flowers, nursery stalls, etc.
  • Soil-less vegetable farming kits with instructions.
  • Sale of flowers, plants, organic items, medicinal plants, compost, fertilizers, organic manure, pesticides, gardening accessories, etc.
  • Various food stalls where people will get to savour the taste of different delicacies.

Why Attend?

Learn basic skills of gardening:

Price: 30$

Planting a garden can be a wonderful hobby. Gardening is a great gentle physical activity that can help older people and people with chronic degenerative conditions stay active and healthy. Gardening is also a great way for active, busy, and very stressed out people to take some time to relax and get back in touch with nature. With this you also get a gardening kit and learn to maintain a garden with basic skills.

Grow Your Vegetables Without Soil:

Price: 45$

A 2 hour program where you’ll get to see how a variety of vegetables can be grown without soil. It will provide participants with the intermediate knowledge and skills to apply hydroponics
(soil-less method) in growing of crops. You will learn important practical skills, how one can grow vegetables under best hygiene conditions by maintaining a residue-free approach.

Autumn and Spring sessions available:

Price: 29$

Come and learn how to revive your small garden, your patio and your pots for the changing seasons. Our mission is to provide quality affordable and accessible lifelong learning opportunities for our community. You will learn all about fertilisers, pruning and diseases so that your next season’s plants will be lush and abundant.

About indoor plants for absolute beginners:

Price: 35$

Indoor plants feature in every picture perfect Instagram post, cafe and at the house of that friend who is perpetually cooler than you. Starting out can be overwhelming and if you pick a plant without knowing what its needs or difficulty level is, you can wind up pretty disappointed when it prematurely dies. This program teaches to grow indoor plants well with basic care and watering.
