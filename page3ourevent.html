John Herman

Co-founder

It doesn’t matter how often you’re knocked down; success comes to those who get up and fight again. John Herman, may have witnessed a few defeats, but his passion for gardening ensured that he constantly strived to regenerate his ideas.

The success of his venture Tipu, a Landscape and Gardening Expo that is held in Auckland once a year. The name is an acknowledgment of your day off and keeping it sacred, spending it well to know more about gardening and some food for your soul. The Tipu is the result of John Herman’s foresight. It is a 15-year-old story. He saw the need for this Expo in Auckland.

William Brady

Gardener

To have been there from the inception of the event – William Brady has seen and dealt with it all . He looks after the execution and liasoning of this expo, with utmost ease.

He is full of life and our “go to person” for everything. He is passionate about any project he takes up and gives it his best. He loves to play golf during his free time which tells us how patient he is too, all in all he is a happy go lucky, peoples person – that’s William Brady for you!

Jessica Anderson

Marketing

With over 15 years of experience in Sales & Marketing and Business Development across many companies in the e-commerce and consumer durables sector, Jessica Anderson is happiest thinking of ways to grow a brand. Focussing on his area of expertise, Jessica has taken charge of Marketing, Sponsorships and Building Alliances for Tipu which has partnered with some good brands. Apart from co-ordinating and churning out promotional campaigns with multiple brands on the Tipu platform, Jessica is particular about building long-term partnerships with each sponsor. Outgoing and effervescent in nature, what Jessica loves most about her job is the chance to meet, interact and learn from people who cross her path – many of whom are considered giants in the world of business and marketing. Proud to be a part of the largest gardening expo in Auckland, Jessica believes that the journey is everything – in life as well as business.

Lucy Adler

Assistant

With over 5 years of experience, Lucy came on board the Tipu team to provide a much essential impetus by way of conceptualizing, executing and expanding business horizons. Therefore with an eye for detail in providing unique experiences Lucy handles all the creative conceptualization, logistical planning and execution for all Events properties. Lucy highlights, encourages and understands the importance in harnessing that talent at Tipu.

As a fitness and sports junkie Lucy embodies the spirit of a healthy balance and transfers this energy to the entire Tipu team!
